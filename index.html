Housewarming Traditions

Griha Pravesh Puja

A sacred ceremony performed to purify the space and invite positive energies into the new home. The priest conducts rituals to bless the house and its inhabitants.

Boiling Milk Ritual

The new homeowners boil milk in the kitchen until it overflows, symbolizing abundance and prosperity. This ritual is believed to bring good fortune to the household.

Vastu Puja

A special worship of Vastu Purush, the deity of directions, to ensure harmony between the house and natural elements. This helps in balancing energies within the home.

Entering with Right Foot

The homeowners enter the new house for the first time with their right foot, considered auspicious in Indian tradition. This is followed by family members and guests.
